Function description:
    This demo is used to test SPI erase/write/read flash model in flash 3byte addr mode.

Test result:
    the test results are printed out via USART PASS/FAIL/TODO.

IO Connection information:
    Connect QSPI1SCK to Flash SCK .
    Connect QSPI1CS  to Flash CS  .
    Connect QSPI1DQ0 to Flash DQ0 .
    Connect QSPI1DQ1 to Flash DQ1 .
    Connect QSPI1DQ2 to Flash DQ2 .
    Connect QSPI1DQ3 to Flash DQ3 .
